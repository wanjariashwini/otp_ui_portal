What is OTP service?

OTP service is the easiest way of verifying user information and authenticating online transactions. Used by businesses to boost data security and prevent phishing attacks, an OTP service provider ensures timely delivery of OTPs to the right customers via SMSes.

How OTP service works

1. Verification request

Customer clicks on the ‘Verify’ button on your app or website and a unique OTP is generated in your backend system

2. Verification

It fetches the generated OTP and sends it to your customer’s registered mobile number (This can be done either through SMSes)

3. Number is verified

Your customer enters the unique OTP sent on your app or website and gets verified instantly

How can businesses use OTP service

1. Two-factor authentication (2FA)

Implement an extra layer of security in your systems, without adding complexity to your customers.

2. Spam prevention

Effectively control spam registrations, and avoid fake signups. Optimize and secure your backend systems against spam.

3. Password Resets

Instead of sending codes or links via email, use the OTP service to verify users before password resets.

4. Passwordless logins

Do away with passwords as your customers can be verified using an OTP every time they log in to your system.

5. Account Reactivations

Reactivate a user account with a confirmed acceptance from them. Use OTP verification as a means of verifying identity.

6. Transaction Authentication

Keep all transactions safe and sound. Add an additional layer of security with OTP verification to ensure safe transactions.
